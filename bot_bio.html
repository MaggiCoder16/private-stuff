— FAQ

Common questions.

How do I switch the AI model?

Use /model and pick from 7 available models. Switching resets the channel's memory for a fresh context. Works per channel and in DMs.

How do I set up moderation?

Run /setup-moderation — you need Ban/Kick/Timeout permissions. The 6-step wizard covers everything in ~2 minutes.

What permissions does Codunot need?

Ban Members, Kick Members, Moderate Members, Manage Messages, Manage Channels.

I already had Codunot — do I need to do anything?

Grant the 5 permissions then run /setup-moderation once.

How do I vote to unlock features?

Vote at top.gg — unlocks image gen, music, TTS for 12 hours.

Voting vs Premium — difference?

Voting (free, every 12h) unlocks image gen, music, TTS. Premium/Gold removes limits and adds /tempban, /massban, /modstats, /note.

How does code testing work?

Use /test_code, paste Python, and Codunot runs it in a sandbox. AI auto-fixes errors and re-tests.

How do I upgrade to Premium or Gold?

DM @aarav_2022 or join the support server.
